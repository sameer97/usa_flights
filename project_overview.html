Project Overview

Have you ever wondered why certain airlines or airports bag the most flights and was this the case from the start or is it a recent trend? USA Flight Data Analysis is your chance to find out. It pulls data from the Data Expo 2009 and offers a simple and intuitive analysis.

Check out the Data section for more information on data, including a quick view of the data summary.

Note

This project was done as part of self learning and its still under progress.

The aim of the project is to provide a graphical summary of important features of the data set and answer some fascinating questions like:

  • What are the most popular airlines in USA?

  • What are the busiest airports in USA?
